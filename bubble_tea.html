Our Story & Passion

At Double Tea, we believe in bringing happiness in every sip. Founded with a passion for authentic flavors and quality ingredients, our journey began with a simple goal: to create the most delightful bubble tea experience.

We source premium tea leaves and the freshest fruits, crafting each drink with care and precision. From classic milk teas to innovative fruit concoctions and delightful toppings, there's a Boba Bliss drink for everyone. Come and taste the difference!
